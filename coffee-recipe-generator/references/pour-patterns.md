# Pour Patterns Reference

| Pattern | Description | Best For |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Center pour** | Water goes straight down middle | Initial bloom, quick saturation |
| **Circular/Spiral** | Slow circles from center outward | Even bed saturation, general use |
| **Zigzag** | Back-and-forth across bed | Large flat beds, quick coverage |
| **Pulse pour** | Pour in bursts with pauses | V60, controlling extraction |
| **Continuous pour** | Steady stream, no pauses | Flat-bottom, Kalita |

## Pour Speed Reference

| Speed | ml/second | When to Use |
|-------|-----------|-------------|
| Slow | 2-4 ml/s | Finer grinds, delicate coffees, bloom |
| Medium | 5-8 ml/s | Standard brewing, most steps |
| Fast | 9-12 ml/s | Coarse grinds, high agitation desired |
