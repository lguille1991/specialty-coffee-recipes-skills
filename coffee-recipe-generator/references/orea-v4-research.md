# Orea V4 — Condensed Research Reference

> **Primary source:** official OREA V4 Guides (`orea.uk/guides-v4`). Use secondary retail or community sources only as discovery leads, and verify claims against OREA or named competition materials before treating them as recipe guidance.

## Overview

The Orea V4 is a modular flat-bottom brewer system supporting 8 distinct brewing configurations across two geometries (Narrow 73° and Wide 65°) and four interchangeable bottom inserts. It is explicitly competition-grade: Martin Wölfl won the 2024 World Brewers Cup (WBrC) with the V4 Narrow + Fast Bottom.

## Two Geometries

| Geometry | Angle | Best For | Notes |
|----------|-------|----------|-------|
| **Narrow** | 73° | Classic flat-bed brewing, competition | Same internal geometry as V3/V2 |
| **Wide** | 65° | Unique cup profile, conical + flat hybrid | Wider bed, different flow dynamics |

## Four Interchangeable Bottoms

| Bottom | Flow Rate | Grind Adjustment | Pour Adjustment | Best Filter | Best For |
|--------|-----------|-----------------|----------------|------------|----------|
| **FAST** | Fastest | Default / slightly finer | Standard spiral pours | Flat paper + Negotiator | Everyday use; washed coffees; WBrC winner choice |
| **CLASSIC** | Medium | Grind coarser | Same spiral pattern | Flat paper + Negotiator | Balanced; natural processed beans |
| **OPEN** | Fast | Same grind | Half circular + half central (except bloom) | Flat or conical | More sweetness; bypass-style recipes |
| **APEX** | Fast | Grind finer | Final 2 pours central only | V60/Origami conical; flat with APEX Negotiator tip | Maximum sweetness/clarity; semi-conical shape |

### Apex Bottom Filter Notes
- **Narrow + Apex:** Use Negotiator with APEX tip to fold flat paper semi-conically. Do NOT use Wave paper with Apex.
- **Wide + Apex:** Use V60-style conical filters (Sibarist Fast Conical for V4 Wide) or fold flat filters with Negotiator.

## Filter Paper Types

| Filter | Flow | Character | Best Pairing |
|--------|------|-----------|--------------|
| **Orea Wave (185)** | Slow | Traditional wave-style | Classic/Open bottom; forgiving |
| **Orea Flat Type G** | Moderate | General purpose flat | All flat bottoms; requires Negotiator |
| **Orea Flat Type C** | Fast | Higher clarity than Type G | Fast/Open bottom; bright coffees |
| **Sibarist Fast Flat** | Ultra-fast | Pre-creased, premium, high acidity | Fast bottom; competition recipes |
| **Sibarist Fast Conical** | Fast | Conical shape for Wide + Apex | Apex bottom in Wide geometry |

## Competition Usage

### Martin Wölfl — 2024 World Brewers Cup Champion (Chicago)
- **Brewer:** Orea V4 Narrow + Fast Bottom
- **Filter:** Sibarist Fast Flat
- **Tools:** WDT (bed prep), Melodrip (gentle pouring)
- **Coffee:** 17g Panama Geisha (Finca Maya, Lost Origin Coffee Lab)
- **Water:** 270ml @ 93°C (Apex Water)
- **Grind:** Medium-coarse, ~630 microns (Comandante C40 ~22-26 clicks)
- **Recipe:** 60ml bloom → 60ml @ 0:40 → 50ml @ 1:20 → 100ml @ 2:00
- **Total time:** ~2:20–2:25

### Other Notable Competitors
- **Matteo D'Ottavio** (UK Brewing Champion): V4 Narrow + Open Bottom, bypass recipe
- **Sherry Hsu (Shih Yuan Hsu)** — 2022 WBrC Champion: While associated with Orea brand reputation in some retail copy, primary sources confirm her 2022 win but do not explicitly state Orea V4 usage (the V4 did not exist in 2022).

## Key Accessories

- **Negotiator / Neo Negotiator (~£17):** Essential for fitting flat filters against walls to eliminate bypass. APEX tip available for conical folding.
- **Melodrip:** For low-agitation pouring; used by Wölfl.
- **WDT Tool:** For leveling coffee bed; used by Wölfl.

## Brewing Principles (from OREA official)
- Dose range: 12–24g ideal (10–28g usable)
- Pour rate: ~5g/s (50g pour in ~10 seconds)
- No pre-heating needed; room temp device is fine
- Dense washed coffees take 20–45s longer than naturals — this is normal and desired
- Recipes designed for light roasts; for darker roasts lower temp and grind coarser
